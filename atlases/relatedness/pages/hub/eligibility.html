Eligibility (p map). Per-window non-crossover (NCO) rate per Mb, computed from population-layer phased haplotypes. NCO is the messenger for the precondition step p: high NCO ⇒ DSB / pairing substrate is plentiful in this window, low NCO ⇒ ancient cold region. Inversion footprints overlaid as coloured strips; the prediction inside a true heterokaryotypic inversion is NCO ≈ flank (sequence-driven DSB competence is preserved).
