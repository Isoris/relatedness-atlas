WHY C, NOT JUST CO RATE
C = x²d / [x(a+d) · x(b+d)] = d / [(a+d)(b+d)]. The exchange probability x cancels, so a focal inversion that only modulates raw CO rate may leave C unchanged. C moves when the precondition / interference structure changes — that is the interesting biology to test with this scan. (See the Meiosis tab Section B for the full observable-vs-latent table.)
