Inversion comparison. Side-by-side view of 2–4 candidates: identity, karyotype distribution + HWE, family-hub confounding, Mendelian Test A p, regime context, marker-readiness. Use when narrowing down which inversions to take into the Pass-2 marker round (10k–50k offspring validation). Read-only — no compute.
WHAT TO COMPARE
  • frequency — too low ⇒ power-limited at scale
  • het count / HWE — heterokaryote deficit ⇒ BDMI Test C fires
  • family-hub share — >80% in one hub ⇒ confound on any contrast
  • Mendelian p — <0.05 ⇒ distortion in DEMO.triads (BDMI Test A fires)
  • marker readiness — frequency ≥ 0.10 AND status=PASS ⇒ ready for Pass-2
