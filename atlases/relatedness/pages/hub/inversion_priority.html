Inversion priority — Pass-1 → Pass-2 bridge. One row per inversion candidate aggregating every signal the other pages compute: intra-chromosomal CO effect (from Inversion-signature), interchromosomal |ΔC| (from Inv × meiosis), Mendelian distortion (from BDMI Test A), family-hub consistency, marker readiness. Composite priority_score ranks candidates; bucket says SHIP → PASS 2 (ready for marker-validation at scale), HOLD (interesting but needs confirmation), or DROP. Use this page to pick the 5–20 inversions you actually take into the next 10k–50k-fish marker round.
BUCKET LOGIC
  • SHIP → PASS 2 = priority_score ≥ 0.55 AND marker_ready AND top-hub carrier share < 80%.
  • HOLD = priority_score ≥ 0.30, or score ≥ 0.55 but not yet marker-ready, or family-confounded.
  • DROP = priority_score < 0.30 — not worth the marker spend.
Priority weights: intra effect 0.30, inter effect 0.25, Mendelian distortion 0.20, family consistency 0.15, marker readiness 0.10. Adjust weights in shared/inversion_priority.js::_compositeScore when the manuscript's emphasis shifts.
