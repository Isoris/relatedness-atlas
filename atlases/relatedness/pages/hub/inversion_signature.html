Inversion signature (cross-layer diagnostic). One chromosome, three stacked tracks — NCO/Mb (population layer), CO/Mb (pedigree layer), and resolution = CO/(NCO+CO). The visual phenotype of a true heterokaryotypic inversion is: NCO ≈ flank, CO ↓↓, resolution ↓↓. Reject as a real inversion when CO is not suppressed (this generation's recombination still ran). Each candidate on the active chromosome gets an inside-vs-flank summary card with one of four verdicts: CONSISTENT / REJECT / ANCIENT COLD REGION / AMBIGUOUS. The karyotype matrix at the bottom identifies the carriers.
