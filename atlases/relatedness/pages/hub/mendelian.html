Test Mendelian segregation for selected dyads (parent–offspring) or triads (parent₁–parent₂–offspring) at every inversion candidate. Computes observed vs expected karyotype proportions and a P-value (binomial for dyads, multinomial-exact / chi-square for triads). Cohort variants run the test across every dyad or triad in the current hub and combine results with Stouffer's method.
